#1 Welcome to LAOS !

 LAOS is an “All In One” mandarin learning toolbox that includes many features and intuitive exercises. LAOS not only offers an elegant pop-up translator but also a personal dictionary and various exercises (such as flashcard, board writing, fill sentences…). All of these, bundled in a minimalist and modern-looking environment for a colourful and engaging learning experience.

NEW: LAOS now provides image recognition translation ! The perfect features to translate subtitles while watching your favorite Mandarin VLOG !!!

Let's get started !

To get all the comfort of using LAOS, simply pin it in your browser top bar to access the settings in a click !
Feel free to switch between Simplified or Tradiontal Chinese. You can even disabled some features such as the Notifications or the Pinyin Bubbles

LAOS main shortcuts

  • R : save a word to your wordlist

  • ALT + E : Open LAOS Center

  • ALT + T: Query your words in Tatoeba to see its usage in sentences

  • alt + S : Start the Image Recognition tool to look-up subtitles from videos

#2 The tools

 LAOS offer many tools to improve your journey to mandarin learning, most of them can be enabled or disabled in the LAOS settings pannel.

Popup Translator

The major feature of LAOS is its popup translator. Simply hover mandarin characters while browsing a web to see its translation and zhuyin writing. You can switch between traditional or simplified Chinese in the LAOS Settings panel.

To add a word to your Personal wordlist simply press the R key.

Pinyin bubbles

Pinyin bubbles acts as little post-it and can be helpful while you are reading an article and wish to pin a specific mandarin word, preventing you of having to hover it again in case you read the sentence again.
To make a bubble appear, simply hover on a mandarin word and click. Click on the bubble to remove it.

You can disable this function anytime via the LAOS Settings panel

Notifications

In order to encourage passive learning, LAOS also includes a Notification system picking randomly a word in your Personal wordlist and displaying it in a Google Chrome notification every 10 or 20 minutes.

You can disable this function anytime via the LAOS Settings panel

Image recognition

The extension has now a Picture recognition system, you can now finally look-up words from your favorite mandarin VLOG!



To start the Image recognition tool press ALT + S
Then simply drag and scale the frame around the word you wish to translate, and press enter

The bigger your frame is, the more time it will take to analyse it.

This tool should be used ONLY on a few words or very short sentences (like subtitles displayed in Chinese videos as instance)



LAOS will then popup a new window with the results on it and its accuracy.




The tool is mostly optimised for simple neutral typefaces, with high contrasts. Thus, it may fail if you try to translate highly stylised characters.

#3 The LAOS Center

On top of providing tools to help you translate chinese characters, LAOS also has a space where you can store and play with your word list to help you remember them: the LAOS Center!
To access the LAOS Center, simply press alt + e or you can also access it through the Settings by clicking the Open LAOS button.
The LAOS Center offers four modes to display your words!

The Worldlist

All the words you saved hover by pressing R are available in your personal word list.
You can click on the rows you to select them and remove them.

Flashcards

For an easy entry level, flashcards are the choice to start learning new words ! Let the mouse flowing through the cards to pair them together.

Fast Type

The Fast Typing mode brings a more intensive (yet efficient) learning pace.

Fill the blanks

For the more advanced, a fill sentences mode is available using Tatoeba.org’s boundless sentences database.
Simply click and drag the boxes on the right and fill the sentences' cases.
